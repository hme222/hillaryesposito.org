Concept: knot-nav at the top → floating bar on scroll

At the top of the page the hero knot's nodes are the navigation. As soon as you scroll past the hero, it condenses into a clean floating bar for the rest of the site. (A real, conventional nav stays in the DOM the whole time for keyboard / screen-reader / mobile — the knot-nodes are a sighted-desktop enhancement.)
